Privacy Policy
At Net Repair, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect personal information from you in the following ways:

Contact Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and home address, which we collect when you request a service, fill out a form on our website, or contact us directly. Service Details: Information related to the appliance(s) needing repair, including the make, model, and issue, to provide accurate service. Payment Information: When you make a payment for our services, we may collect billing information such as your credit card number and billing address. All payment transactions are encrypted and processed securely. Website Data: We collect information about how you use our website, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing patterns through cookies and similar technologies. This data helps us improve the user experience. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your personal information to:

Provide and Manage Services: Your information helps us to process service requests, schedule repairs, and follow up on completed services. Process Payments: We use your payment details to charge you for our services securely. Customer Support: Your contact details allow us to respond to your inquiries, provide troubleshooting assistance, and address concerns. Improve Our Services: Website data and feedback help us enhance our services, improve our website, and ensure we meet your needs effectively. Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may send you promotional offers, updates on our services, or other marketing communications. You can opt out of receiving these communications at any time.

3. How We Protect Your Information
We take your privacy seriously and employ various security measures to protect your personal information, including:

Data Encryption: All sensitive information, including payment details, is encrypted during transmission using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. Restricted Access: Only authorized employees and contractors with a legitimate need to know are given access to personal information. Data Retention: We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party providers who assist us in delivering services, processing payments, or maintaining our website. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for other purposes. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, investigate fraud, or ensure the safety of our employees and customers.

Opt-Out: If you no longer wish to receive marketing communications, you can opt-out by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or contacting us directly. 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us analyze web traffic and personalize your experience.

Types of Cookies: We use both session cookies (which expire once you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until you delete them). Your Choices: Most web browsers allow you to control cookie settings and block cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For more information on our use of cookies, please see our Cookie Policy.
